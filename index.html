Rules & Regulations

Follow the fair play spirit of the game!

College Requirement

All team members must be from the same college.

Player Eligibility

Each player can represent only one team. Playing for multiple teams leads to disqualification.

Team Format

7 players + 3 substitutes per team; rolling substitutions allowed.

Match Time

10-minute halves; in case of a tie ‚Üí 3 penalties.

Gameplay

No offside rule; no slide tackling.

Goalkeeper Rules

Goalkeepers cannot throw the ball past the half line, cannot take volleys, and must release the ball within 4-6 seconds.

Required Documents

Students must bring original Aadhar Card and College ID Card for verification.

Document Verification

Teams submitting false or invalid documents will be disqualification of team.

Throw-ins & Restarts

All throw-ins and outside balls must be taken from a grounded kick.

Discipline

Yellow card ‚Üí 3 minutes out; Red card ‚Üí suspended for current & next match.

Conduct

Only captains may speak to referees. Misconduct leads to team elimination.

Zero Tolerance

Any fights or serious misconduct will result in immediate team elimination.

Referee Decision

The referee's decision is final and binding in all matters.
